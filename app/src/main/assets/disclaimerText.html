The routedata will be used for collection of data  about bike routes. The data will be available as open data in an anonymized way. The anonymized data will be available as open data under the CC0 license, or a CC0 compatible open-source license (see https://creativecommons.org/about/cc0/).

The data will be anonymized as follows:

  • 1. All links to personal data (name, email, ip address) will be not sent to any external system;
  • 2. The startpoint and endpoint of your route will be changed by another point along the route (the exact startpoint and endpoint can’t be derived);
  • 3. The recording time of your route will be categorized in a 30min slot (the exact timing can’t be derived).

By publishing your route as a public route, you agree that the route data will be published. Your credentials will be saved on the server to make it possible to delete your recorded routes. By publishing a route no personal information will be displayed to any other users.

Data about road issues will be stored on the server together with your contact information.
